Další informace najdete v části „Co když mi vypršela platnost platební karty a platba je po splatnosti?" v článku [Platba předplatného Office 365 pro firmy](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054).  </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00a8c-p101">If you pay by credit card, and your credit card is declined, you'll receive an email letting you know that we were unable to process the payment. You can update your credit card information and immediately submit the payment by using the **Pay now** button on the [Subscriptions](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page. For more information, see "What if my credit card has expired and my payment is past due?" in [Pay for your Office 365 for business subscription](https://support.office.com/article/734f4aab-df2d-4e9b-8cb1-691910bde216).</span></span>
